Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat using the formula:
bodymass divided by the square of body height





A person with a BMI of less than 20 is underweight;
more than 25 is overweight. If your BMI is in the range of 20 to 24, then your bodymass is in the acceptable range

Disclaimer note: These calculations are for adults. The BMI for children is calculated in the same way but the interpretation is different.
